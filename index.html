Welcome 

Modern Technology Company , LTD (MOTECO) is a Saudi Arabian and regional leader in wireless/Wireline Telecom Solutions, satellite telecommunications, electromechanical works and construction services, providing specialized, customized solutions for government and military.

Vision

We aim to rehabilitate, modernize and secure organizations across all industries against the advanced threats and attacks in today’s world. Acting in partnership with organizations, we will provide unmatched information telecom systems and security services designed to improve your overall networks posture, close gaps and track vulnerabilities on an ongoing basis through continued rebuilding and support.

Mission

We establish partnerships with our clients based on trust and results. We leverage our deep industry knowledge and expertise to identify and remediate your systems, provide meaningful visibility and value of your entire enterprise, and align your organization with security best practices and compliance standards.

About Us
Modern Technology Company, LTD – MOTECO is a local and regional leader in providing Wireless/Wireline Telecom Solutions, Satellite telecommunication, Electromechanical works and Construction services as well as Medical Equipment and Diverse bespoke Solutions. We partner with clients in all sectors and regions to identify their highest value opportunities, address their most critical challenges, and provide them with cutting edge solutions and services. Using our industry know-how technology expertise and our ambitious vision, we are able to provide our clients with optimized services and solutions to achieve results in a record time with world class quality and standards. This ensures that our clients achieve sustainable competitive advantage, build more capable organizations and secure lasting results. The company was launched in 2001 to give a rapid response to many of our customers who we have enjoyed extensive relations with since 1980.
