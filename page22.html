Social Responsibility

23 April 2019

ARMED FORCES

MOTECO hosted a number of officials from the Communications and Information Technology departments of the Armed Forces as well as some government agencies in Nice, France, to demonstrate Airbus' latest technologies in tactical communications, critical communications and cyber security.

2 April 2025

Al-Wafa Women Philanthropic Association

MOTECO has sponsored Al-Wafa Women Philanthropic Association, which is dedicated to supporting families, providing maternity care, educating needy families, educating women about their role in society, and providing financial assistance to needy families.

9April 2025

Saudi Parkinson Society

MOTECO made a donation to Saudi Parkinson Society, a non-profit association that helps Parkinson's patients through deep brain stimulation implants, physiotherapy and the provision of smart tools and medicines.

23 April 2025

Hail Turbah Auction For Camels

MOTECO sponsored the Hail Turbah Auction for Camels for the 2019 championship of Arabian Purebred Camels, which was held in the city of Hail under the patronage of His Royal Highness Prince / Abdulaziz bin Saad bin Abdulaziz Al Saud, Governor of Hail.

23 April 2025

King Fahd Security College
